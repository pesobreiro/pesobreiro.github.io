Call for papers

 

Speakers are invited to submit proposals for papers to be present at the conference. Papers may be written from an management and economic perspective, and should address issues relating contemporary sport management and economic area.

If you are interested in presenting a paper, please supply an abstract with 150 words until 17 Maio 2002, (cbarros@iseg.utl.pt). Notification of acceptance will be (e)mailed by the scientific committee until 31 Maio.
