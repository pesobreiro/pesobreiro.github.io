Getting Started: Creating Your Project

The Getting Started tutorial helps you complete your first project plan in no time. It examines the four main phases of the project management process and guides you step by step through procedures for creating and managing your project plan. Key concepts that help you perform your project management tasks effectively are clearly explained and illustrated. Unlike the linear design of many tutorials (which forces you to read through the tutorial screens in a strictly determined order), this tutorial’s modular design lets you get to exactly the information you want when you want it.
