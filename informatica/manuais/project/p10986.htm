Refine Task-Duration Estimates

The more accurate the information in your project plan, the more accurately Microsoft Project can schedule tasks. That’s why it’s so important to take one more look at your task-duration estimates. Can some tasks be accomplished in less time? Realistically, does your experience and intuition tell you that some tasks will take longer than you’ve estimated? Using the best information available, put any new or improved task-duration estimates into your project plan.
