Part 2: Putting Your Project Plan on the Fast Track

Focusing on the beginning of project plan creation, you learn how to start a new project and add the most basic building blocks in any plan: tasks and resources. You find out how to enter tasks, organize tasks logically, and put them in the sequence you want. Specific procedures show you how to assign resources to tasks, set resource working days and hours, and assign costs to tasks and resources.
