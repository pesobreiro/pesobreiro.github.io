Check Costs vs. Budget

If you’ve entered task and resource costs in your project plan, you can check your total project cost. If it’s over budget or unacceptably high, you’ll need to look at individual task and resource costs to see if you can reduce them. For example, you might be able to cut the scope of a task or replace an expensive resource with a less costly one.
