Part 6: Connections That Count

Many projects today are conducted by teams scattered around the country, if not the globe, who use a variety of programs to store, calculate, and edit project information. To help you manage complex projects, even several at the same time, the chapters in Part 6 explain how to exchange project information with your team through an e-mail system or the World Wide Web, share information with Microsoft Office and other programs, and combine several projects into one central project plan.
