Formatting the Gantt Chart

In addition to using the GanttChartWizard, you can apply formatting directly to any selection to make your Gantt Chart look just the way you want. You can use the Formatting toolbar to enlarge field text or to make it bold, italic, or underlined. You can change the appearance of a Gantt bar by simply double-clicking the bar and then selecting the formatting options you want.
