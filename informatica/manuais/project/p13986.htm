View the Total Cost of a Resource

Because resources are often a project’s biggest expense, an effective way to reduce project cost is to reduce resource costs. To determine which resource costs to reduce, first display the total cost of each resource.

To view the total cost of a resource

  1. On the View Bar, click Resource Sheet .
  2. On the View menu, point to Table, and then click Cost.
  3. Examine the Cost field to determine the total cost of the resource.
